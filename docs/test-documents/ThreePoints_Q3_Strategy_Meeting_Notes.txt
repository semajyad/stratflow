=== KEY STRATEGIC DECISIONS ===

1. MARKET EXPANSION — AUSTRALIA AND SOUTHEAST ASIA

Martin outlined the expansion strategy: "We've proven the model in New Zealand. Australian enterprises have the same pain points — slow strategy-to-execution cycles, misaligned teams, and no visibility into strategic drift. Our StratFlow platform solves all three."

Target markets:
- Australia (primary): Focus on Sydney and Melbourne enterprise accounts. Target companies with 500-5000 employees in financial services, healthcare, and technology sectors.
- Singapore (secondary): Gateway to Southeast Asia. Target government-linked enterprises and MNCs with regional headquarters.
- Philippines (tertiary): Offshore development partnerships and BPO companies seeking to improve their delivery processes.

Key milestones:
- April 2026: Establish Australian subsidiary (ThreePoints Australia Pty Ltd)
- May 2026: Hire Regional Sales Director (Sydney-based)
- June 2026: Launch first 3 Australian enterprise pilots
- August 2026: Singapore market entry with 2 strategic partnerships
- October 2026: First revenue from Australian accounts

Budget allocation: $1.2M for market expansion (travel, hiring, marketing, legal entity setup)

2. STRATFLOW PLATFORM ENHANCEMENT

James presented the StratFlow V3 roadmap. Current platform handles strategy document ingestion through to sprint allocation. V3 adds enterprise-grade capabilities:

Priority features (in order):
a) Jira/Azure DevOps bidirectional sync — "This is the #1 request from every enterprise prospect. Without it, we can't close deals over $50K ARR." (James)
b) Executive dashboard with portfolio view — "The person signing the contract needs to see ROI. They don't care about user stories." (Lisa)
c) Microsoft Teams meeting integration — "Every strategy starts in a Teams call. If we can ingest meeting transcripts directly, we eliminate the first friction point." (Sarah)
d) Real-time collaboration — comments, @mentions, notifications — "We need multi-user before we can sell to teams larger than 5." (David)
e) AI Strategy Coach — conversational interface for on-demand strategic advice

Technical dependencies:
- Jira sync requires OAuth 2.0 implementation with Atlassian's API
- Teams integration requires Azure AD app registration and Microsoft Graph API access
- Dashboard requires Chart.js integration and a caching layer for performance
- All features must maintain HIPAA/SOC2 compliance — audit logging on every action

Timeline: Q3 for Jira + Dashboard, Q4 for Teams + Collaboration
